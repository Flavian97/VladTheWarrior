Storyline

In this section I am going to talk about the storyline for my text adventure game. The action of my text adventure game is going to be split in two parts one is going to take place in Romania after the second World War in a city called Bran where long time ago use to live Dracula. In fact, in this location we can find the Dracula’s Castle which is very famous around the world. And the second part is going to take place in Russia in Moscow. The main character of my storyline is called Vlad who has 23 years old. A brave teenager who wants to build a name in this little town and not only as he is not enough satisfied how people recognise him after the war. His life was a chaotic one because he lost both of his parents during the second World War. His character is quite harsh, a teenager who suffered a lot but who can hide all the suffer and hate. The life and the pressure situations that he went through though to him how to be calm and find the right way in order to cope with the difficult situation. He doesn’t like to talk too much he likes to go straight to the point. During the second world war he manage to earn a lot of medals as he was able to complete all the given missions successfully. After the second World War he was not enough satisfied considering that he lost all the members of his family, even if he managed to win a lot of medals during this time. He wanted that the people would recognise him as a hero but in front of them he has nothing special probably because of his background his family was extremely poor and nobody had a little respect to him and his family that is the main reason why he wish to change this aspect so much and being seen by other in a different way. One day, after two years when he came back in town, he has received a letter from his commander where the commander is thanking him for the good work that he has done in the war. Furthermore, the commander is having a special mission for him in case if he wants to accept it. The mission consists in finding the Romanian Treasure which basically is a collection of many different items, jewels in gold and gold reserves here we are talking about full waggons of gold that belong of the Romania government that has been sent in Russia for safekeeping and which never been returned. From this letter Vlad understood that this is his big opportunity to show not only to the people of his town who is him but also to the entire nation. Therefore, in the end he accepted the mission and next day he went to meet the commander for further details. When he arrived next day to meet the commander. The commander started to go in details regarding the treasure. Consequently, from the resources that the commander received the treasure is located near Moscow in Russia and that this is an extremely hard mission because usually all the best soldier that the commander sent there in order to get more information regarding the treasure no one managed to came back so the commander told him to pay attention at any details as he is going to be a spy in Moscow and being able to find precise information with the exact location of the treasure. Once Vlad manage to identify the treasure and provide enough evidence for the portfolio that the commander has, the commander will be able to request the treasure from the Russia government as is written in the contract.3 In conclusion Vlad will go to Russia in Moscow and face different challenges in order to be able to find the tressure and provide the evidence required which allows the commander to request back the treasure.
